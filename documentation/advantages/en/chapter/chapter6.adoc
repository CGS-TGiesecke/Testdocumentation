=== Document Management & RAG for Customer Documents

*{application}*

* Dedicated Documents tab with DMS-like functionality:
** Filter by document type (PDF, Excel, etc.), source (network drive, SharePoint, etc.), and content
** Keyword-based search using stored keywords
** Upload function for new documents into the RAG – even without technical knowledge
** Refresh RAG via "Refresh" button
** Delete individual documents via "Delete selected embeddings"
** Display and review embeddings per document

Advantage:

* RAG maintenance becomes so easy that even users without AI expertise can keep the company’s knowledge base,
ifeval::[{arc-assist} == 1]
Internal Audit
endif::[]
up to date.

*MS Copilot / ChatGPT*

* No integrated RAG-DMS interface for company-specific document landscapes
* Integration of company documents is possible, but:
** less granular control
** technically and administratively more complex
ifeval::[{arc-assist} == 1]
** not explicitly designed for audit workflows
endif::[]
