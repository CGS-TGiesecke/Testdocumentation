=== Description 

{application} (hereinafter Assist) provides a powerful AI platform that enables organizations to leverage their knowledge intelligently and turn it into productive outcomes. Through specialized chats and preconfigured use cases, employees gain fast and precise access to relevant company information—whether sourced from internal documents or cloud‑based repositories.
Assist streamlines the analysis of complex content, simplifies the formulation of inquiries, and enables efficient data processing. In this way, artificial intelligence becomes a true driver of productivity in everyday work.

Upon first login, users are informed that the application uses artificial intelligence to automatically generate content. 
The output is based on probabilities and may contain errors. Users are asked to carefully review the results. 
This notice must be acknowledged in order to continue using the assistant.

image::../images/Abbildung-confirm.jpg[Navigation - KI confirmation, title="Navigation - KI confirmation", width=500]

Certain regular use cases are managed as templates within Assist to simplify routines. The use cases are designed like a kind of checklist.
With the appropriate permissions, automated, scheduled queries on automatically provided information sources, known as feeds, can be created.

The themed navigation areas can be accessed via a navigation bar at the top edge.
Depending on the user's permissions, the number of entries and the design may vary.

image::../images/Abbildung-1.jpg[Navigation sections, title="Navigation sections", width=500]

Subpages of these navigation areas can then be accessed via the tree navigation on the left edge.

image::../images/Abbildung-2.jpg[Navigation sections - tree navigation, title="Navigation sections - tree navigation", width=500]

Additionally, a symbol at the upper right edge allows for selecting the language within the application and switching the view to dark mode or back again.
The user profile can also be viewed, and it is possible to change the personal password.

image::../images/Abbildung-3.jpg[Selecting the language and switching the view, title="Selecting the language and switching the view", width=500]

Within the entire application, colored notification dialogs are displayed in the upper left corner when do actions.
The colors indicate the notification level as follows:

|===
|green	| Successful execution of the action
|blue	| General information about the action
|red	| Error during execution of the action
|===
