
==== Navigation Area "Portal"

This page, and therefore the corresponding menu item, can be completely enabled or disabled in the administration settings.
If the page is enabled, all links to other pages or applications that are stored in the administration and are available 
to the user are displayed here, provided the user has the necessary permissions.

image::../images/Abbildung-40.jpg[Navigation area - Portal, title="Navigationsbereich -area", width=400]

By clicking the icon, the link will be opened in a new browser tab or window, depending on the browser settings.
