
==== {application} integreted DMS

This is a PostgreSQL database connection in {application}, where system-defined folders and customer-maintained documents are stored.
Folders are created in the database via script according to the customer's requirements. They are initially visible to all users. 
The customer is responsible for setting the required role permissions for the folders.
Storage of partial documents (embeddings) takes place in an internal vector store (ChromaDB).

Files are uploaded manually by the customer. Permissions on individual files correspond to those of the respective directories. 
All data storage and access management are defined and managed by the customer.
