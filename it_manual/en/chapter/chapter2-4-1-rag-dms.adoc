
==== {application} integreted DMS

This is a PostgreSQL database connection in {application} where all folders and easily maintainable documents are stored by the customer.
The required permissions for the roles on the folders are set by the customer.
The storage of the document fragments (embeddings) takes place in an internal vector store (ChromaDB).

Files are uploaded manually by the customer. Permissions on individual files correspond to those of the directories.
All data management and access management is defined and administered by the customer.
