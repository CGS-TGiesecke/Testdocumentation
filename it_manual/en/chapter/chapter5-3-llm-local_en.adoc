
==== Local Models (for Highest Data Protection Requirements)

*For customers who do not want any cloud connection.*

The platform {application} supports local LLMs via *OpenAI-compatible APIs*.

*Supported tools:*

- *Ollama* (the simplest solution) 
- *vLLM* (highest performance) -
- *LM Studio* (GUI-based) 

*Recommended models:*

- *Llama* (very good quality) 
- *Qwen* (excellent, multilingual)
- *Mistral* (high quality)
